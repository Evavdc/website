Taplokaal

The Taplokaal, an old building located in Delft once served as a factory. It is a historic structure composed of four sections, the oldest dating back to 1880. Currently, the building stands empty and dilapidated. For the bachelor's final project at TU Delft, the task was to design a pop stage for this site. To seamlessly integrate these elements, the Taplokaal was extended with a fifth section, reflecting the form and rhythm of the original building.
