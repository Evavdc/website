Melkhal

Located in the eastern part of Boddenkamp (Enschede), the former Coberco site, named after the dairy factory that closed its doors in 1995, holds historical significance. Today, only the perimeter buildings and the iconic production hall known as "De Melkhal" remain standing amidst the demolition of the rest of the industrial complex. During the internship at IAA Architecten, involvement included designing various elements for both the interior and exterior of the Melkhal and constructing a 1:100 scale model to facilitate communication with the client.
