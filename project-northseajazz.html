North Sea Jazz

An annual art competition, held in collaboration between the North Sea Jazz Festival and WdKA (Willem de Kooning Academy), invited submissions to design a poster for the festival in Rotterdam. My final design, inspired by Rotterdam's iconic harbor with its stacked shipping containers, was selected as one of the 15 artworks showcased at the North Sea Jazz Cafe. The design creatively integrated the festival's identity onto the containers, reflecting both the vibrancy of Rotterdam and the dynamic spirit of the event.
