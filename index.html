ABOUT ME

Iâ€™m Eva van der Cammen, an emerging architect and spatial designer with a passion for creating thoughtful, functional, and sustainable spaces. I began my academic journey studying Spatial Design at the Willem de Kooning Academy in Rotterdam, where I graduated in 2020. Eager to expand my architectural knowledge, I completed a premaster program at Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), followed by a Master of Architecture, which I graduated cum laude. During my Master's, I developed a strong interest in working with existing structures, focusing on transformation, renovation, and the adaptive reuse of buildings, particularly monuments and heritage sites. This growing fascination has shaped my design approach, blending respect for historical context with innovative and sustainable design strategies. My work brings together creative vision and practical design solutions, always grounded in a commitment to enhancing the human experience. I aim to craft spaces that are both meaningful and enduring where past, present, and future come together through thoughtful architectural design.
